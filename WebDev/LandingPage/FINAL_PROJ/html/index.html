Our History


ReGift Your Old Gift was created in 1986 in Cleveland, Ohio. The first location was on Euclid Avenue. Business was slow, as the store opened in the summer, before the holidays. But once January came around, business was booming. People from all over the state were coming in to sell or exchange their gifts. in 1987, one year after the inital opening, we opened a second store on Exchange Street in Akron. There was demand for this location for those in the Akron-Canton area. business grew at a steady pace and soon, we were opening one to two stores a year in Ohio. In 1990, we expanded outward to Indiana and Pennsylvania. From there, we kept growing and expanding. In 2003, the first version of the ReGift Your Old Gift website launched. it's been 20 years since we launched this website and we thank you all new and returning customers year over year to give gifts to those who want a knock off on the cheap or make some money selling those pairs of socks you keep saying you don't need because you have too many, but your family doesn't know you well enough to get you anything else of meaning.

Thank You, everyone.

And Here's To 20 More Years!
